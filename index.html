Meet The Artist

Harriet Boucher

I am a watercolour artist based in London with an eye for detail and intricacy. I have always enjoyed painting architecture, from the lines and details, to the varying colours. I love being able to capture this through my work and portray the colour and detail to create unique paintings of your beloved homes

"We are so in love with this painting of our beloved home" - The Woods, 2022

Being based in London, I am able to visit local locations to get a feel of the environment, colours, and details, as well as take my own photographs to work from. However, I am also happy to work from your high quality photographs, when working on homes further afield

"The best Christmas present for my husband!" - Anna Boucher, 2021

To make a commission even more special, I am able to arrange Personalised Printed A6 correspondence cards and framing. If this is something you would like to arrange please get in touch.

"So intricate and beautiful" - The Brocks, 2023
